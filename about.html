About me

A lifelong artist, I have worked with a bunch of different mediums from acrylics to charcoal to watercolor. I'm always looking to try something new or master a different technqiue.

Lately, I've become fascinated with Impressionism. I have always been into photorealism as an artist. I wanted to really go outside my comfort zone. The works on this site are my forays into Impressionism.
